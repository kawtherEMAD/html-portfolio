The Best Movies According to Kawthar

My top three movies of all-times

Just Go with it

Romantic/comedy movie starring by Adam Sandler and Jennifer Aniston. One of my best comfort movies, which is very hilarious and fun to watch.

Lion

Heartbreaking movie about a 5-year-old Indian kid who lost his way home and ended up being adopted by an Australian couple. When he grew up, he decided to look for this real family and meet them again.

Interstellar

science fiction mind-blowing movie that makes you think of your purpose in life and value it.
